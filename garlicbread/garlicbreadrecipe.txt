List of Steps -
Part One: Make the Dough
			1. Add warm water to the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook. 
			2. Sprinkle sugar and yeast into the water. Let this stand for 5 to 10 minutes until it is foamy. 
			3. Mix in butter, milk, salt, and stir flour in gradually.
			4. Knead for 7 to 10 minutes.
			5. Divide dough into two equal portions.

Part Two: Shape the Dough & Make Topping
      1. Using a small bowl, combine the butter, garlic, salt, and parsley.
			2. Cut up your dough and dip them in the topping.
			3. Arrange the dough balls into one regular loaf pan.
			4. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour. Reserve the leftover butter mixture.

Part Three: Bake the Bread
			1. Bake at 350°F (176°C) for 30 minutes or until golden brown. 
			2. Paste the butter mixture on the pull-apart garlic bread after they are out of the oven.

Ingredients

  • pick tea bag of your choice (2 cups)
  • let 1 liter of water boil in the kettle for 5 min
  • pour one cup of water into a mason jar and let one tea bag seap in the jar for 2-3min
  • add two tbsp of sugar & stir
  • cut 2-3 slices of lemon and place one as granish on the side of the cup and the rest in the jar/li>
  • than get a handful of ice and place in the jar
  • add straw
